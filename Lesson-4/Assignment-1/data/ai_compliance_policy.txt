Presidio AI Data Handling and Compliance Policy

Employees using AI systems must ensure:

1. Customer PII is never exposed to external AI systems.
2. AI-generated outputs must be reviewed by human supervisors.
3. All AI interactions are logged for auditing.
4. Models must comply with GDPR, HIPAA, and SOC2 policies.
5. Internal AI systems must use encrypted storage.
6. AI recommendations cannot be directly deployed to production without validation.
7. Sensitive insurance claim data must remain within approved enterprise systems.

Violations may result in disciplinary action.
