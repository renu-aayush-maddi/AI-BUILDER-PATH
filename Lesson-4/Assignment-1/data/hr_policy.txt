Presidio HR Policy Manual

Working Hours:
Employees are expected to work 9 AM to 6 PM Monday through Friday.

Remote Work:
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.

Annual Leave:
Employees receive 24 paid leave days annually.

Sick Leave:
Employees receive 12 sick leave days annually.

Performance Reviews:
Performance evaluations occur twice per year.

Code of Conduct:
Employees must maintain professional behavior and comply with company ethics.

AI Usage:
Employees must not upload confidential client data into public AI tools.

Data Privacy:
All customer information must comply with GDPR and CCPA requirements.
